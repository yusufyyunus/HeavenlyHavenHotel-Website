About Us

 Our History
Heavenly Haven Hotel was founded in 2003 by Yusuf Yunus. It started as a small boutique hotel nestled in the serene countryside, offering a peaceful retreat for weary travelers seeking solace and relaxation. Over the years, it has grown into a renowned destination known for its luxurious amenities, impeccable service, and breathtaking surroundings.
Our Team

 Founder/Owner: Yusuf Yunus- The visionary behind Heavenly Haven Hotel, passionate about providing guests with a tranquil and rejuvenating experience. 

General Manager: Hazim Ahmad- Responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of the hotel, ensuring that guests receive top-notch service and that the staff works efficiently. 

Hospitality Team: Comprising receptionists, concierge staff, housekeeping, and other personnel dedicated to ensuring guests' comfort and satisfaction during their stay. Culinary Team: Talented chefs and kitchen staff tasked with creating exquisite culinary experiences for guests, ranging from gourmet meals to delectable snacks and beverages. Maintenance and Groundskeeping: Responsible for maintaining the hotel premises, ensuring everything is in pristine condition from the lush gardens to the well-appointed rooms. 

Marketing and Sales Team: Works to promote the hotel, attract new guests, and enhance the hotel's brand image through various marketing strategies and initiatives.

Mission And Vission
At Heavenly Haven Hotel, our mission is to provide guests with an unforgettable escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. We strive to create a sanctuary where they can relax, recharge, and indulge in the ultimate luxury experience. Our commitment to excellence in service, attention to detail, and dedication to guest satisfaction sets us apart as a premier destination for discerning travelers seeking tranquility and refinement.
